## 🎯 The Problem
Relationship Managers were experiencing extreme cognitive overload due to fragmented systems, legacy software, and manual data aggregation. Preparing for a single client meeting required navigating **5 different systems** and manually consolidating data into Word, Excel, and legacy PowerPoint templates. This friction directly reduced the amount of time advisors could spend fostering deep human connections and growing their book.

## 💡 Core Design Principles
1.  **Workflow Before Data:** Moved away from plain "database-first" grid dumps. Every interface is built to prioritize and answer temporal questions (e.g., *"What is my morning pulse at 9 AM?"*).
2.  **AI Augments, Never Replaces:** AI acts as a high-fidelity co-pilot, drafting summaries and preparing recommendations, but keeping the human advisor in complete control (review, edit, and approve).
3.  **Trust Through Transparency:** Every AI-generated recommendation surfaces clear inline confidence ratings, detailed reasoning, and one-click access to source provenance.

## 🚀 Key Solutions & Features
*   **Household-First Architecture:** Shifted the root entity from scattered individual "Accounts" to consolidated "Households", reducing click-depth for primary operations by 60%.
*   **Meeting Mode:** An AI-driven preparation pipeline that automatically aggregates performance metrics, goal progress, and drafts a personalized quarterly narrative in under 30 seconds.
*   **Predictive Risk & Mandate Triage:** Early warning horizons overlaying a 92% confidence band to visual mandate boundaries, allowing RMs to resolve drift inline in one click.
*   **NLG Client Statements:** Natural Language Generation (NLG) that instantly summarizes a 40-page financial PDF into a 3-paragraph plain-English overview for HNWI clients.
*   **Knowledge Graph "Ask AI":** Instantly surfaces relationship context across 10 years of historic meeting notes, eliminating client succession friction.
