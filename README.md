## Overview
This repository contains a comprehensive UX/UI Case Study detailing a 24-week project to redesign the Strand Finance private wealth management platform. 

The core philosophy of this project was to treat AI as a fundamental material woven directly into the user's workflow—not as a bolted-on feature. By strategically placing AI as a synthesis layer between raw financial data and the interface, the redesign radically transformed how Relationship Managers (RMs) operate.

## 📈 Key Impact Metrics
*   **Pre-meeting Prep:** Reduced from **2 hours down to 30 seconds**.
*   **Drift Detection:** Shifted from reactive (post-breach) to **predictive (7 days ahead)**.
*   **Time Reclaimed:** **30%** of the RM's week was freed up for direct client-facing work.
*   **Client NPS:** Doubled from **32 to 64**.

## 🎯 The Problem
Relationship Managers were experiencing extreme cognitive overload due to fragmented systems, legacy software, and manual data aggregation. Preparing for a single client meeting often required navigating 5 different systems and manually consolidating data in Excel and Word. This reduced the time RMs could spend actually building relationships.

## 💡 Core Design Principles
1.  **Workflow Before Data:** Moved away from "database-first" grid dumps. Every interface now answers a temporal question (e.g., "What needs my attention at 9 AM?").
2.  **AI Augments, Never Replaces:** AI acts as a co-pilot, drafting narratives and surfacing insights, but always allowing the RM to review, edit, and approve.
3.  **Trust Through Transparency:** Every AI-generated insight provides inline reasoning and one-click access to source provenance.

## 🚀 Key Solutions & Features
*   **Household-First Architecture:** Shifted the root entity from "Accounts" to "Households", reducing click-depth for common tasks by 60%.
*   **Meeting Mode:** An AI-driven workflow that automatically aggregates data, drafts a narrative, and formats a presentation in 30 seconds.
*   **Predictive Risk & Compliance:** Early warning systems that surface portfolio drift and KYC updates directly on the daily dashboard.
*   **NLG Client Statements:** Natural Language Generation (NLG) transforms dense 40-page financial PDFs into accessible, 3-paragraph summaries for clients.
*   **Knowledge Graph "Ask AI":** Allows RMs to instantly query 10 years of meeting context and relationship history.
