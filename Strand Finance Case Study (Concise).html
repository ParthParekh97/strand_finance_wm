13 / METHODOLOGY & TECH
DESIGN TO DEVELOPMENT
PROCESS & HANDOFF

From user insights to Angular components.

1. User-Centered Design Process

Generative Research & Synthesis
Shadowed 12 RMs and collaborated with Quant teams to translate complex financial datasets (risk, TWR, volatility) into decision-focused interfaces.
Iterative Prototyping
Progressed from low-fi Information Architecture (IA) tests to high-fidelity dashboards. Validated task flows cut interaction depth by 60%.
Accessibility & Inclusion (WCAG)
Enforced AAA contrast for all financial data. Implemented screen-reader friendly data tables and colorblind-safe palettes for variance indicators.

2. Engineering Collaboration

Angular Component-First Design
Created a scalable, modular design system for the Angular engineering team. Delivered clear specs for states, transitions, and lazy-loading chart behaviors to handle large datasets.
Power BI Integration Constraints
Designed advanced visualizations (e.g., Variance Analysis, KPIs) mindful of Power BI's custom visual SDK limitations, ensuring high fidelity without breaking dashboard performance.
Cognitive Load Management
Utilized progressive disclosure—separating global KPIs from deep drill-downs—so RMs see actionable insights immediately without being overwhelmed by data density.
